Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a password. After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

How can I add movies to my watchlist?

Simply click the bookmark icon () on any movie thumbnail. The icon will turn orange to indicate it's been added to your watchlist. You can access your watchlist from the navigation menu.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! Movizi is available on both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store. Our mobile app offers all the features of the website with additional mobile-exclusive content.

How often is new content added?

We update our library weekly with new releases and classic films. TV series are typically updated within 24 hours of their original airing. You can enable notifications to be alerted about new additions.

Can I download movies to watch offline?

Yes, with our Premium subscription you can download movies and shows to watch offline. Downloads are available for 48 hours after you start watching or 30 days after download (whichever comes first).

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime by going to your Account Settings and selecting "Cancel Subscription." Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period, and you won't be charged again.
